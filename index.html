Understanding
Health Score
Calculated automatically on save. Shown as a badge at the top of the form. You cannot edit it — it reflects your inputs honestly.
75–100
🟢 Healthy
Project is on track. Keep going.
50–74
🟡 At Risk
Some areas need attention. Review with your manager.
0–49
🔴 Critical
Immediate action required. Escalates to leadership.
ℹ️Score is derived from Project Status, Schedule Confidence, Client Satisfaction, SETU Compliance, and Team Morale. Fill all fields honestly for an accurate score.
Final Step
Submitting
Once all 6 tabs are filled, submit to lock the review and publish to the leadership dashboard.
1
Save at any point — stores a draft, not visible on dashboard yet
2
Review all 6 tabs and verify everything looks accurate
3
Click Submit — review locks and appears on the dashboard immediately
4
Your reporting manager is notified for sign-off
⚠️Once per project per month only. To correct after submitting, ask your manager to cancel it.
